Warranty — Lenovo US
====================

Lenovo Limited Warranty:
Most Lenovo hardware products come with a one-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

Think-branded products (ThinkPad, ThinkCentre) often come with an extended 3-year limited warranty for business users.

Premium Care & Legion Ultimate Support:
Customers can purchase Lenovo Premium Care or Legion Ultimate Support for extended coverage, 24/7 technical support, and on-site service beyond the standard warranty.

Battery & Accessory Warranty:
Regardless of the system warranty length, the warranty for batteries and accessories (like pens and styluses) is strictly limited to 12 months.
