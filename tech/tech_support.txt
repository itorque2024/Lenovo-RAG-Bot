🛠️ Lenovo Technical Support & Recovery Knowledge Base
=====================================================

This document provides comprehensive technical procedures for troubleshooting, repairing, and configuring Lenovo hardware and software.

1. Self-Service Repair (CRU)
----------------------------
Lenovo provides "Customer Replaceable Units" (CRUs) for many of its products. These are parts that can be replaced by the user without voiding the warranty.
- **Self-Help Manuals:** Access model-specific Hardware Maintenance Manuals (HMM) on the Lenovo Support site for step-by-step disassembly instructions.
- **Parts Ordering:** Genuine Lenovo parts can be ordered via the "Parts" section on pcsupport.lenovo.com by entering your system serial number.

2. Software Tools & Diagnostics
-------------------------------
### Lenovo Vantage
- **Purpose:** The primary dashboard for managing your Lenovo PC.
- **Features:** Hardware scans, system updates (BIOS/Drivers), and warranty status checks.
- **Usage:** Pre-installed on all Lenovo Windows PCs; also available on the Microsoft Store.

### Lenovo Diagnostics (UEFI/Windows)
- **Purpose:** Deep hardware-level testing of CPU, RAM, Storage, and GPU.
- **Access:** Press **F10** during the Lenovo splash screen to launch the built-in UEFI diagnostics.

3. Recovery & OS Reinstallation
-------------------------------
### Digital Download Recovery Service (DDRS)
- **Purpose:** Create a USB recovery drive to restore your PC to its factory-original state.
- **Requirements:** 16GB+ USB drive, your system’s serial number, and a Lenovo ID.
- **Process:** Visit `support.lenovo.com/lenovorecovery` to request a download link.

### Emergency Reset Hole
- **Purpose:** For laptops with internal batteries that become unresponsive (won't turn on or wake up).
- **Procedure:** Use a straightened paperclip to press the reset button located in a small hole on the bottom of the laptop for 10 seconds.

4. BIOS/UEFI & Boot Management
------------------------------
| Task | Key Sequence |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Enter BIOS Setup** | Press **F1** or **F2** repeatedly at startup. |
| **Boot Menu (Select Drive)** | Press **F12** at startup. |
| **System Recovery** | Press **F11** or use the **Novo Button** (small button with a curved arrow icon on the side of the chassis). |

### Flea Power Drain (Hard Reset)
If the system fails to boot:
1. Unplug the AC power adapter.
2. Hold the **Power Button** for 30–60 seconds.
3. Reconnect the AC adapter and attempt to turn it on.
